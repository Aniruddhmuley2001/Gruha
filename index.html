About


Gruha is an attempt to make property searching easier and efficient in times, when we are able to find only a handful of homes on the internet, and are not so satisfied with the options available. Not a conventional place, where you will deal with traditional brokers and loose excess money in commissions. In the cities of our service, we try to cover each and every place, conduct surveys, and list down the best places, to help you find your ideal homes.
